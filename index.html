About Me Development Jukebox

Hello! I'm Karan Tyagi.
I'm a software engineer working at Intuit Credit Karma. At Intuit Credit Karma, I focus on building scalable, data-driven features that simplify financial decisions for millions of users. My work bridges the gap between complex backend logic and intuitive front-end experiences, particularly within the Easy Apply / Lightbox Platform.

Currently, I am focused on:
- Integrating AI-driven capabilities into our platforms to create more conversational and accessible user interfaces.
- Optimizing the application lifecycle by architecting logic layers that reduce friction.
- Collaborating across cross-functional teams to deliver high-impact integrations, such as the Lightbox Everywhere initiative with QuickBooks.

With a background in Software Engineering and Data Science, I am passionate about leveraging full-stack development and Machine Learning to build impactful, user-centric products.
