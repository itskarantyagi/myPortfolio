About Me Design Development Jukebox

Hello! My name is Karan Tyagi. I'm a software developer. I am a creative person but have learned to be technical over the years.

I am currently pursuing my Masters in Software Engineering with Specialisation in Data Science from San Jose State University. During my bachelors I explored Computer Graphics and have previously interned at Double Negative(DNEG) and Tau Films.

Since January, I had been working as a Research Assistant at Indian Institute of Science(IISc), Bangalore under the supervision of Prof. Pradipta Biswas and Abhishek Mukhopadhyay where I got the opportunity to got exposed to the field of Computer Vision. I actively contributed to a research project on Lane detection for self-driving car. This experience motivated me to pursue a specialization in Data Science. I am also interested in AR/VR.
